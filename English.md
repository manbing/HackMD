* Two to the exponent of n, written as 2<sup>n</sup>, is the number of ways the bits in a binary word of length n can be arranged:
> 2 to power of n

wait await difference
---
the main difference is that `await is a transitive verb that requires a direct object and is used in formal writing, while wait is a more general verb that can be used alone or with a preposition like "for" and is common in everyday speech`. Wait can also function as a noun, while await does not. 

Transitive Verb(及物動詞)
---
及物動詞的英文是 transitive verb，通常簡寫為 Vt. 或 V.t.。這類動詞需要接一個受詞（Object）來完整表達句子的意思，否則語意會不完整。 
* 英文術語：transitive verb
* 縮寫：Vt. 或 V.t.
* 句型結構：主詞 + 及物動詞 + 受詞
範例：
I eat breakfast.（我吃早餐。）
She bought a new phone.（她買了一支新手機。）
Kevin loves sneakers.（Kevin 喜歡運動鞋。）
* 辨別技巧：可以試著在動詞後方問「誰？什麼？」，如果能直接回答並構成受詞，通常就是及物動詞。
She reads (什麼？) → a book → 是及物動詞。
